# Madame-Bubble-LT-GameJam-2025 

**Madame Bubble** is a point-and-click puzzle game created for LT GameJam 2025.

You play as a mysterious fortune-teller and potion brewer, visited by clients with strange, vague, and sometimes very literal problems.  
Your task is to understand what they *really* want… and brew the right potion for them.

---

## About the Game

Each client arrives with a story — sometimes clear, sometimes cryptic.  
It’s up to you to:

- carefully read and interpret dialogue and context
- figure out the client’s true intention
- gather ingredients
- brew potions
- observe the outcome

---

## Gameplay

- Point-and-click interactions
- Context-based puzzle solving
- Ingredient collection and combination
- Potion crafting based on interpretation
- Multiple possible outcomes

---

## Core Idea

People don’t always say what they truly want.  
Sometimes you need to be a fortune teller…  
and sometimes a therapist.

---

## Controls

- Left Mouse Button — interact with objects
- Hover — context hints / tooltips
- Drag & Drop — combine ingredients in a cauldron

---

## Features

- Atmospheric mystical aesthetic
- Text-driven interpretation puzzles
- Unique clients with mini-stories
- Multiple solution paths
